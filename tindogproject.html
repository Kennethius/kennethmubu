The Tindog website is a conceptual dating website for dogs. The goal was to create a website like Tinder, Bumble, and other dating sites/apps. However, this came with a twist. It was designed specifically for dog owners who are able to connect with other dog owners. It would serve as a dual purpose for owners to find companionship for their dogs and to find friendship/companionship with other dog owners.
