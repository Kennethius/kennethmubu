- PROJECT

Spotter

The Spotter app is a navigation app coupled with a mood reader in the form of a ring. The goal was to an app that is capable of providing you with constant on-the-go updates on events taking place around your city based on your mood. The website was re-designed to make it intuitive and user friendly. See blog for detailed information. The app, Spotter, is a concept app that was initially created to make planning for social life and individual activites much easier.
